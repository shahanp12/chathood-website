Enterprise Integrations

Connect Chathood to powerful tools and services to extend your communication platform.

Webhooks

Trigger external workflows by sending HTTP callbacks. Connect Chathood to any service or CRM with real-time webhooks.

Dialogflow

Use Google Dialogflow to build intelligent chatbots. Map your Dialogflow agents to channels for natural language interactions.

Google Translate

Automatically translate incoming and outgoing messages in real time, so agents and customers can chat in their native languages.

OpenAI

Leverage ChatGPT and other OpenAI models for smart message suggestions, automated replies, and content summarization within your inbox.

LeadSquared

Sync contacts and leads between Chathood and LeadSquared. Centralize customer data and automatically log interactions in your CRM.

RealtimeKit (Voice & Video)

Add voice and video calling capabilities to your chat. RealTimeKit powers in-app calls, conference, and screen sharing for support teams.

Dashboard Apps

Embed external analytics dashboards and widgets directly in Chathood. Connect BI tools, reporting apps, or any web portal for in-context insights.

Automation Frameworks

Integrate with third-party automation platforms (Make, Zapier, etc.) to create complex workflows that span across Chathood and your other enterprise systems.
