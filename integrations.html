Powerful Integrations

Extend Chathood into a complete CPaaS ecosystem using APIs, automation tools, CRMs, and AI services.

Webhooks

Trigger real-time events whenever a message is received, updated, assigned, or resolved. Seamlessly connect Chathood with your backend systems, CRMs, ERPs, or custom applications.

Dialogflow

Build intelligent conversational bots using Google Dialogflow. Handle FAQs, collect user inputs, qualify leads, and route conversations to agents when needed.

Google Translate

Automatically translate incoming and outgoing messages in real time. Enable multilingual support so agents and customers can chat in their preferred language.

OpenAI

Use OpenAI models for AI-powered reply suggestions, conversation summaries, sentiment detection, and automated responses inside the inbox.

LeadSquared

Sync leads, contacts, and conversation activity between Chathood and LeadSquared CRM. Ensure sales and support teams always have up-to-date customer data.

RealtimeKit (Voice & Video)

Enable voice calls, video calls, and screen sharing directly from the chat interface for high-touch support and sales use cases.

Dashboard Apps

Embed external dashboards, analytics tools, and internal web apps directly inside Chathood for contextual insights and faster decisions.

Automation Platforms

Connect with platforms like Zapier, Make (Integromat), or custom automation engines to orchestrate workflows across your entire tech stack.

API-First by Design

Chathood is built for developers, CPaaS providers, and system integrators.

REST APIs

Access conversations, contacts, messages, agents, and automation using secure REST APIs.

On-Behalf Messaging

Send messages on behalf of users or systems using WhatsApp Business API, SMS, or other channels.

Enterprise Integrations

Integrate with CRMs, ticketing systems, ERPs, payment gateways, and internal platforms.
